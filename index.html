Over 50 Years of Excellence

Since our founding, Western Cactus Enterprises has been dedicated to growing the highest quality cacti and succulents for wholesale distribution. Our decades of experience have allowed us to perfect our growing techniques and develop efficient systems for mass production.

Located in Vista, California, our facilities are designed to provide optimal growing conditions for these specialized plants. We take pride in our ability to consistently deliver healthy, vibrant specimens that meet the needs of retailers, landscapers, and businesses nationwide.

Our team of experienced growers and horticulturists are passionate about what they do, ensuring that every plant that leaves our nursery meets our rigorous quality standards.
